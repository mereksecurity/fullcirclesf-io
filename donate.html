Support Our Work

Your tax-deductible gift helps restore Santa Fe's land and enrich its community. Every dollar goes to work in the high desert.

"Donations to Full Circle fund native plant restoration, community workshops, public art installations, and the volunteer programs that make all of it possible."

Every dollar you give is an investment in Santa Fe's ecological health, its cultural vitality, and the generations who will inherit this extraordinary landscape. We steward your gift with care and transparency.
