Our Team

The people behind the mission — passionate stewards of land, community, and culture.

Leadership

Our Founders

Executive Director

Joe

Joe brings over 15 years of experience in ecological restoration and nonprofit leadership. A Santa Fe native, he founded Full Circle to reconnect the community with the land that has shaped the region's culture for centuries. His background spans native plant horticulture, watershed management, and community organizing.

Creative Director

Chas

Chas merges his passion for Southwest art and culture with a deep commitment to community-driven design. With roots in both gallery arts and public installations, he ensures that beauty and cultural meaning are woven into every Full Circle project. He has collaborated with over 20 local artists on public art commissions.
