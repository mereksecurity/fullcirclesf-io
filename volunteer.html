Volunteer With Us

Your time and talent help restore Santa Fe's land, strengthen its community, and celebrate its culture.

Get Involved

Every Hand Makes a Difference

From pulling invasive plants to painting murals, from knocking on neighbors' doors to organizing spreadsheets — Full Circle runs on the energy of volunteers who care deeply about this place. Whatever your skills, whatever your schedule, there is meaningful work here for you.

Volunteer Opportunities

🌿

Landscape Steward

Work side-by-side with our restoration team to plant native species, remove invasive plants, and maintain restored sites across Santa Fe. No experience necessary — just a willingness to dig in.

⏱ Every other Saturday, 3–4 hours
🤝

Community Outreach

Help us connect with neighbors, community organizations, and local businesses. Share our story, gather input on upcoming projects, and recruit new volunteers and supporters.

⏱ Flexible, 2–5 hours/week
🎨

Art Installation Assistant

Support our Creative Director and local artists in preparing and installing public art works. Help with site preparation, logistics, materials, and community events around installations.

⏱ Project-based, 1–2 weekends
📋

Administrative Support

Help keep our small team running smoothly with data entry, grant writing assistance, social media support, donor communications, and event coordination.

⏱ Remote, 3–5 hours/week
