What We Do

Our Three Pillars

Everything we do flows from three interwoven commitments — to the land, to our neighbors, and to the living culture of Santa Fe.

🌿

Native Landscaping

We restore native plant ecosystems across Santa Fe — replacing invasive species with drought-adapted flora that supports local pollinators, reduces water use, and heals the high-desert landscape.

🤝

Community Networks

We build meaningful connections between residents, neighborhoods, and local organizations — fostering a spirit of collective stewardship and shared pride in Santa Fe's public spaces.

🎨

Local Art & Culture

We celebrate Santa Fe's extraordinary artistic heritage by commissioning public installations, partnering with local artists, and weaving cultural meaning into every restoration project we undertake.

Featured Initiative

Santa Fe River Restoration

The Santa Fe River — once the lifeblood of the region — has suffered decades of neglect and degradation. Full Circle is leading a multi-year initiative to restore native riparian habitat along the river corridor, reintroducing willows, cottonwoods, and native grasses that once defined the landscape.

Phase 2 of the project is now complete, with over 400 native plants established and critical erosion control measures in place. We're now raising funds for Phase 3, which will extend restoration to the upper watershed.
